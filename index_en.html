What does Keep in run consist?

Keep in run is a game “endless runner”, that is, an infinite game where you have to avoid obstacles.The protagonist, a student in the class of Tinf, is bored, he looks out the window and imagines that is flying, alien to his evil teacher. The goal is to entertain themselves during all the class. To do this, there is an indicator of boredom which is filled with each marker that you don't cross. The evil teacher will put impediments, such as forcing you to throw the gum or remove material from other subjects with rare excuses which will increase your level of boredom.
